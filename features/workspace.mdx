A **workspace** is the central place where you and your team build apps together in ZenOpus. Every project lives inside a workspace, and every member collaborating with you accesses projects through a workspace.

Think of it as your team’s home base. It holds your projects, your people, your billing, and your settings in one place.

When you sign up for ZenOpus, your first personal workspace is created automatically on the **free plan** with a 5 daily credit allowance. From there you can:

- Invite others to join
- Create projects
- Upgrade your plan as your needs grow

You can also create additional workspaces from the **workspace switcher** in the top-left corner of the dashboard.

## Workspace basics

These are the key rules that define how workspaces function in ZenOpus.

- **Projects belong to a workspace**  
All projects exist inside a workspace.
- **Pricing plans are workspace-level**  
Each workspace has its **own plan and billing**. A paid subscription applies to **one workspace only**.
- **Credits are shared across the workspace**  
All projects in a workspace share the workspace’s plan and credits. This applies regardless of which member sends messages.
- **You can belong to multiple workspaces**  
A single ZenOpus account can be a member of multiple workspaces. You can switch between them at any time.

## Create a workspace

Any logged-in user can create a workspace.

1. Open the **workspace switcher** (top-left of the dashboard)
1. Click **Create workspace**
1. Enter a name (up to 50 characters)
1. Click **Create** and set up your pricing plan

## Workspace setup

Workspace owners and admins can configure settings, [billing](/introduction/plans-and-credits), and security options for their workspace.

### Workspace settings

Workspace **owners and admins** can customize workspace settings under **Settings → Workspace**.

Available settings include:

| Setting | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Avatar | Set a workspace icon. |
| Name | Your workspace display name (maximum 50 characters). |
| Workspace handle | A unique handle for the workspace profile page. |
| Default monthly member credit limit | The default monthly credit limit for all members of the workspace. Leave empty to use no limit . You can configure specific member limits from Settings → People . |

To manage plans and billing, go to **Settings → Plans & credits**.

### SSO and SCIM user provisioning

**Just-in-time provisioning (Business and Enterprise)**  

 Workspaces with [SSO](/features/business/sso) configured can enable **just-in-time provisioning**. When a user with a verified domain email logs in through the SSO provider for the first time, they are automatically added to the workspace with a configurable default role.

**SCIM provisioning (Enterprise)**  

 Enterprise workspaces can provision users using the [SCIM protocol](/features/business/scim). This allows your identity provider to automatically:

- Create users
- Update memberships
- Deactivate workspace memberships

## Invite people to your workspace

Workspace members are the people you collaborate with on projects in a workspace.

Editors and above can invite members to the workspace, either by email or by sharing an invite link.

### Invite by email

<Note>
On **Enterprise plans**, you can choose to only allow **admins and owners** to invite people by email. Go to **Settings → Privacy & security → Restrict workspace invitations.**
</Note>

1. Go to **Settings → People**
1. Click **Invite members**
1. Enter one or more email addresses
1. Select the role:
    - Owner
    - Admin
    - Editor
    - Viewer
1. Click **Invite**

When selecting roles:

- Only **owners** can invite other owners
- Assigning roles such as **admin or viewer** requires a **paid plan**

### Invite via link

<Note>
Enterprise workspaces do not support invite links. On all other plans, admins and owners can disable invite links entirely from **Settings → Privacy & security → Invite links.**
</Note>

1. Go to **Settings → People**
1. Click **Invite link**
1. Choose the role:
    - Editor
    - Viewer (available on paid plans)
1. Click **Create invite link**
1. Copy the link and share it

Invite links expire after **5 days** and can be regenerated or deleted at any time.

- Only **one active link per role** is allowed at a time.
- Creating a new link replaces the previous one.

## Manage workspace members and invitations

Go to **Settings → People** to manage workspace members.

From here you can:

- Add new members via email or invite link
- Revoke an invitation
- Change member roles
- Remove members
- View monthly credit usage per member
- Set individual member credit limits
- Perform bulk membership actions: change roles, set credit limits, or remove members

Available actions depend on your role.

## Join a workspace

Once you have a ZenOpus account, there are several ways to join a workspace.

### Email invitation or invite link

A workspace owner, admin, or editor sends you an invitation by email or sends you an invite link.

If **auto-accept invitations** is enabled in your personal account settings, you are added to the workspace without needing to manually accept the invites.

### Find your team’s workspace

If you signed up with a **work email address**, ZenOpus can automatically surface workspaces that belong to your organization. This helps you join the correct workspace without needing a direct invitation.

<Note>
This feature is available to accounts using **non-public email domains** (such as `@yourcompany.com`). Public domains like `@gmail.com` or `@hotmail.com` are not supported.
</Note>

**How to find a workspace:**

1. Open the **workspace switcher** (top-left of the dashboard)
1. Click **Find workspaces** at the bottom of the dropdown and ZenOpus will search for workspaces associated with your email domain
1. Select the workspace you want to join
1. Choose the role you want to request
1. Submit your request

Workspace admins and owners will be notified and can **approve or deny your access**.

<Tip>
If you do not belong to any workspace yet, this option also appears in the **No workspace** dialog shown after sign-up.
</Tip>

## Switch workspaces

Open the **workspace switcher** in the top-left corner of the dashboard and click a workspace name to switch to it.

## Leave a workspace

You can leave a workspace from **Settings → Workspace**.

<Warning>
Owners cannot leave the workspace unless another owner exists.
</Warning>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="How many members can one workspace have?">
Workspaces support **unlimited members** on all plans. For Enterprise workspaces, custom member limits can optionally be set.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Can I have several workspaces?">
Yes. However, each workspace requires its **own subscription**.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Can I switch between workspaces?">
Yes. Go to the main dashboard and click your workspace name in the top-left corner. In the dropdown you will see the other workspaces you have access to. Select one to switch.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Can I change the workspace owner?">
Yes. Owners can promote other members to **owner**. A workspace can have **multiple owners**.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
