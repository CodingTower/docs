## Who can contact Support?

Support is available to all of our paying customers. Only requests originating from ZenOpus account email addresses that are part of a paying workspace will receive official support responses.

## Scope

ZenOpus’s support covers the following areas:

- Billing issues
- Platform bugs
- Account management
- Workarounds or solutions to known issues
- General guidance on using ZenOpus
- General questions about ZenOpus

ZenOpus’s support does not cover:

- Project-specific debugging
- Guidance with implementation of third-party or open-source integrations

## Channels

To contact support, you must go through the support form found [here](https://zenopus.dev/support). If the form is unavailable, you can also email support at [support@zenopus.dev](mailto:support@zenopus.dev).
After you’ve submitted your request, you’ll receive a near instant AI response to your inquiry.
If that doesn’t resolve your issue or answer your question, you can request to be routed to a human agent.

All users, free and paying, can access our vibrant and helpful community in our [Discord](https://discord.gg/zenopus-dev) for community support.
ZenOpus team members and champions may participate in community discussions, but official support is only guaranteed through the above-mentioned channels.

For platform-wide issues, updates will be posted on our [status page](https://status.zenopus.dev/).
