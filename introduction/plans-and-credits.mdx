ZenOpus is a subscription based service with a free plan and several paid plans. When you pay for a subscription you get access to more features, and more credits. You need credits to send messages in ZenOpus.

## Feature comparison: Free plan vs. Paid plans

### Free plan

- 5 daily credits, up to a maximum of 30 per month
- Workspace collaboration with unlimited members
- Private projects

### Pro plan

- 5 daily credits up to a maximum of 150 per month
- Monthly credits depending on your plan
- Workspace collaboration with unlimited members
- Workspace roles and permissions
- Private projects
- Ability to connect custom domains to your ZenOpus projects
- Ability to remove the “Edit with ZenOpus” badge from your ZenOpus projects
- Access to Code mode to edit code directly inside of ZenOpus

### Business plan

*Everything in Pro, plus:*

- SSO
- Restricted projects within workspaces
- Opt out of data training
- Build reusable design templates to streamline your projects

## Credits

Credits are used when sending messages to generate output in ZenOpus. The cost depends on message complexity.

Your workspace receives credits from your plan, daily allocations, and optional one-time top-ups. This section explains how credits work, how they’re used, and how to get more when you need them.

### Credit display

To view how many credits you have or have used, select your workspace name on the main dashboard or the project name when inside the project editor. You’ll see how many credits you have left this billing period, as well as the credit bar. The credit bar provides a visual representation of how many credits a user has remaining and how many have been used.

- **Grey part**: Shows the amount of credits already used this billing period.
- **Blue parts**: Shows different types of remaining credits. Hovering over each colored section will display a tooltip indicating the credit type and the exact number of credits available for that type.

### Credit usage

ZenOpus has a usage-based credit system, where sending messages deducts credits. The cost of a message depends on its complexity to ensure you only pay for what you actually use.

Many messages cost less than 1 credit, while more complex ones may cost more. This approach allows for more precise edits and greater efficiency per message, making ZenOpus more affordable overall.

Here are some example prompts and their cost:

| User Prompt | Work done | Credits used |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Make the button gray | Changes the button styles | 0.50 |
| Remove the footer | Removes the footer component | 0.90 |
| Add authentication | Adds login and authentication logic | 1.20 |
| Build a landing page with images | Creates a landing page with generated images, a theme and sections | 2.00 |

## View credits used

Click the three dots beneath any message to see the cost of each message in the chat history.

### Credit top-ups

Credit top-ups are available on **Pro and Business plans** and let you buy **one-time additional credits** without changing your subscription. They’re useful if you run out of credits mid-cycle or have occasional spikes in usage.

Top-ups do not affect your monthly plan, billing cycle, or subscription tier.

You can buy credit top-ups from:

- **Settings → Workspace → Plans & credits**
- The **low-credits alert in chat** when you’re running out of credits

**How it works**

- Credits are purchased in **50-credit increments**
- Minimum purchase: **50 credits**
- Maximum purchase: **1,000 credits**
- Multiple purchases are allowed
- Credits are added to your workspace instantly
- Pricing depends on your plan:
    - Pro: $15 per 50 credits
    - Business: $30 per 50 credits
- Top-up credits are valid for **12 months** from your most recent purchase

**Billing**

- Credit top-ups are billed as a **one-time payment**
- Payments are processed via **Stripe**
- An **invoice or receipt** is generated for every purchase

<Tip>
If you consistently need more credits each month, upgrading your plan is usually better value.
</Tip>

### Credit rollovers

All unused monthly credits automatically roll over at the end of each billing cycle for both monthly and annual paid plans, as long as you keep an active subscription. The rolled over credits will be available for one month on the monthly plan. For users on the **annual plan**, the rolled over credits will be available for the rest of the time on your annual plan.

**Cancellation**: Upon cancellation of a paid subscription, all unused and rollover credits will expire at the end of the current billing period and will not carry over.

<Warning>
Daily credits do not roll over. Unused daily credits will **not accumulate** from day to day for the free tier or for the paid tiers.
</Warning>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="What is a credit?">
Credits are used when sending messages in ZenOpus. The number of credits used depends on the complexity of the message.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="How can I upgrade my subscription?">
1. Go to **Settings → Workspace → Plans & credits**, or visit [this page](https://zenopus.dev/?settings=billing).
1. Choose your desired amount of credits in the credits per month dropdown and then click “Upgrade”. Complete the upgrade in the Stripe portal.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="How can I downgrade my subscription?">
1. Visit [this page](https://zenopus.dev/?settings=billing) or go to **Settings → Workspace → Plans & credits.**
1. Choose your desired amount of credits in the credits/month dropdown and then click “Downgrade”.
1. Complete the downgrade in ZenOpus and then in the Stripe portal. Your current plan will remain active until the end of the current billing period.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="How can I cancel my subscription?">
1. Visit [this page](https://zenopus.dev/?settings=billing) or go to **Settings → Workspace → Plans & credits.**
1. Click Manage → Downgrade to Free, and complete the steps. Your current plan will remain active until the end of the current billing period.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="How can I change my billing information?">
To change your billing information, go to **Settings → Workspace → Plans & credits → Manage → Edit billing information.** You’ll be redirected to the Stripe portal where you can change your information.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="How do I download my invoices?">
Go to **Settings → Workspace → Plans & credits → Manage → Invoices & Payments**. You’ll be redirected to the Stripe portal where you can find past invoices.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="How do I get more credits?">
To get more credits, go to **Settings → Workspace → Plans & credits**, where you can upgrade your plan or purchase a one-time credit top-up.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="When does my monthly credit limit reset?">
Your monthly credit limit will reset when your billing period resets. This happens every month on the same date that you started your subscription. For detailed date and time, check your [billing section](https://zenopus.dev/?settings=billing).
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="When does the daily credit limit reset?">
Daily credits are being reset every day at midnight UTC. On a free plan you can use a total of 30 free credits per calendar month.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Can I buy a top-up of credits?">
Yes. If you’re on a **Pro or Business plan**, you can buy one-time credit top-ups without changing your subscription.

See **Credit top-ups** above for details on pricing, validity, and purchase options.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="How do I see the remaining credits in a workspace?">
You can see the remaining credits by selecting the workspace icon top left of the dashboard, or by selecting the project name while in the project editor.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Can I limit how many credits a user can spend in my workspace?">
Yes, on **Business and Enterprise plans**, workspace owners and admins can set monthly credit limits for individual users:

1. Go to **Settings → People**.
1. Click the three-dot menu at the end of a user’s row.
1. Select **Set credit limit**.
1. Enter the monthly credit limit in the modal and save.

When set, the user cannot exceed that credit allowance within a billing period.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Can I get free credits?">
We do **not offer free credits** through support requests.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Common issues and troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="I can't pay for my subscription. What should I do?">
Occasionally, payment setups may fail due to issues with Stripe. If this occurs, double-check your payment details and retry. If the problem persists, please try using a different payment method.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="I've paid but still don't have access to my plan. What should I do?">
Please reach out to support on [support@zenopus.dev](mailto:support@zenopus.dev).
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="I've upgraded but haven't received any credits?">
You will receive credits only if you upgrade to a plan with a higher credit allowance. If you have done so and still haven’t received your credits, please reach out to support on [support@zenopus.dev](mailto:support@zenopus.dev).
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
