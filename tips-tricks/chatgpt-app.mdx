The **ZenOpus ChatGPT App** lets you create a new ZenOpus project directly from a ChatGPT conversation. By tagging `@ZenOpus`, your prompt and conversation context are transformed into the initial build of a working application.

![ChatGPT conversation interface showing the @ZenOpus tag being used to build an app from a conversation](/images/zenopus/tips-tricks/chatgpt-app/zenopus-chatgpt-app.png)

Use ChatGPT to refine your idea before building. You can brainstorm features, explore different approaches, and clarify requirements in natural language. The more specific your conversation, the better your first app build will be.

When you are happy with the direction, tag `@ZenOpus` and ask it to turn the conversation into a working application. This workflow lets you focus on product thinking in ChatGPT while ZenOpus handles the technical implementation.

## Key capabilities

- Build full-stack applications directly from ChatGPT conversations
- Turn ideas, notes, and discussions into interactive prototypes
- Create landing pages, internal tools, and MVPs without writing code
- Go from concept to working app in minutes

## How to use it

Follow these steps to start building with the ZenOpus ChatGPT app:

<Steps>
<Step title="Plan in ChatGPT">
Log in to [ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com) and describe what you want to build. Outline the purpose, target users, key screens, and core functionality to clarify your product idea.
</Step>
<Step title="Invoke the ZenOpus app">
Tag `@ZenOpus` in your message and ask it to build your app. For example:

```text
@ZenOpus Turn this into a real working website.
```
</Step>
<Step title="Connect the app (first time only)">
The first time you use ZenOpus in ChatGPT, you will be prompted to connect the app so it can access your conversation context and start the build.
</Step>
<Step title="Iterate in ZenOpus">
The ChatGPT app creates the initial project from your conversation. Open ZenOpus to edit, customize, and publish your application.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>

</Tip>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="How do I connect the ZenOpus app in ChatGPT?">
Tag `@ZenOpus` in ChatGPT and ask something like `Turn this into a real working website.` The first time you do this, ChatGPT prompts you to connect the ZenOpus app so it can use your conversation context and start the build.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="When do I get charged ZenOpus credits?">
You are not charged ZenOpus credits until you send the prompt to ZenOpus and it begins to build from the prompt. Exploring ideas and tagging `@ZenOpus` in ChatGPT doesn’t use ZenOpus credits.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Can I continue editing my ZenOpus project in ChatGPT?">
No, for the initial release you need to open ZenOpus to continue editing your project. The ChatGPT integration creates your starting point, and iteration happens in ZenOpus.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
