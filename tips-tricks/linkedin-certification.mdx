## What are vibe coding levels?

Vibe coding levels track how actively you use ZenOpus. Your level increases automatically based on the number of messages you send while building, with no setup required.

Levels are based on real usage and activity, rather than formal assessments or tests. You can view your current level in **Settings → Your account → Vibe coding level**.

![Vibe coding level user interface in account settings](/images/zenopus/tips-tricks/linkedin-certification/linkedin-vibe-coding.png)

## How vibe coding levels work

Vibe coding levels are calculated based on messages sent on ZenOpus.

- Every prompt and iteration counts
- The more you build, the higher your level
- Levels update automatically as your activity grows

Each level represents a different stage of engagement:

- **Bronze**: Getting started with ZenOpus
- **Silver**: Regular ZenOpus user
- **Gold**: Active ZenOpus builder
- **Platinum**: Highly engaged ZenOpus builder
- **Diamond**: Top-tier ZenOpus engagement

## Add your vibe coding level to LinkedIn

You can optionally display your vibe coding level on your LinkedIn profile.

To connect LinkedIn:

1. Go to **Settings → Your account → Vibe coding level.**
1. Click **Add to LinkedIn.**
1. Connect ZenOpus to your LinkedIn account.

When connected, your level appears publicly on LinkedIn under **Licenses & certifications**. Your LinkedIn entry updates automatically as your level changes.

Your level is only shared if you choose to connect, and you can disconnect at any time. If disconnected, the entry is removed from LinkedIn.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Does my vibe coding level expire?">
No. Your vibe coding level reflects your ongoing activity on ZenOpus and updates automatically as you continue building. There is no expiration date.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Can my vibe coding level go down?">
No, your level is based on total usage over time and updates as your activity grows. It does not decrease due to periods of inactivity.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Can I remove the certification from LinkedIn?">
Yes. You can remove it at any time by disconnecting LinkedIn from **Settings → Your account → Vibe coding level** or directly from your LinkedIn profile.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="What happens to my LinkedIn certification if I delete my ZenOpus account?">
If you delete your ZenOpus account, the certification remains on LinkedIn unless you manually remove it.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
